Call for Abstracts
Michigan State University & University of Michigan invite you to their joint symposium on Data Science and Women+ 2020

The 2020 Data Science and Women+ Symposium, jointly organized by the Michigan State University (MSU) and the University of Michigan (U-M) will take place on April 17 at MSU. We cordially invite abstract submissions for lightning talks and poster presentations.

The symposium provides a platform for all data scientists and students, but especially women and gender minority participants, to discuss cutting-edge research, network, get to know resources for research and career advancement. Individuals of any gender are welcome to attend.

We invite abstracts in all of the following areas:

  • Theoretical foundations of data science
  • Methodology and tools for data science
  • Real-world applications of data science in any research domain
  • Ethics, fairness, and societal impact of data science
  • Emerging areas of data science

Submission Deadline: Feb. 29, 2020
Notification: March 15, 2020

Submission Instructions:

  • Please submit your abstract through the online form.
  • Please include a title, list of authors/presenters and their affiliations.
  • At least one author should be affiliated with either MSU or U-M.
  • To celebrate women and gender minority data scientists on your team, please indicate their roles in the project (securing research funding, project design, data collection, analysis, writing, project management, project supervision).
  • We strongly encourage the women and gender minority members of your team to be the presenters of the talks or posters.
  • The main body of the abstract should be no more than 300 words, and should include a brief summary of the research, methods, main results and impact.
  • Please do not include figures, tables or bibliography in the abstract.
  • Please indicate whether you prefer an oral presentation or poster. Oral presentations will be 10 minutes in length, including Q&A. The review committee will try its best to accommodate your preferences.
